✡ Aries ✡

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, symbolized by the Ram. Aries belongs to the element of fire, its ruling planet is Mars⚦. Mars means "burning" and "bright". Aries is the first fire sign that starts the triad of fire signs (like Leo and Sagittarius. Aries is a fire that has just been born, like the first flame tongue from the fire. Aries comes first to set the idea, to make it public, and to bring that idea for perfection with the flame of other zodiac signs.

Strengths: active, determined, confident, enthusiastic, optimistic, honest, passionate, courageous, self-confident, loves risk, full of energy.

Weaknesses: moody, short-tempered, impulsive, aggressive, carefree, uncompromising, impatient, conflicting, ruthless, selfish.

Aries came into this life to prove that his opinion should be considered. Aries men are more persistent in achieving their goals than women. Women can easily give up upon first unsuccessful attempt. But people of such sign always have the opportunity to succeed, the question is only in what way they are going to use. Notice that this is a General caracteristic of the sign. to understand what kind of Aries is next to you, and what qualities prevail, you need to watch a personal horoscope.
