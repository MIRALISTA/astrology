✡ Aquarius ✡

She symbol of Aquarius is a man holding 2 jars of living and dead water. Water is sensitivity and mysticism. Another symbol of Aquarius is a woman carrying a jug. It is impossible to exist without water, and this sign represents the inexhaustible source of life. Probably, the main idea of the mission of Aquarius is to bring a fresh stream in our life.

The air is the element of Aquarius, which determines a significant mental activity. Aquarius are mostly able to learn easily. They usually are smart and love ancient history, philosophy, mysticism and astrology. Aquarius highly support tolerance and complete freedom. They can be very vinegarish when express their opinion. Aquarius have absolute, impenetrable confidence in their own right, it is almost impossible to change their mind. Despite the fact that Aquarius are very sociable, they are not flexible

Aquarius sign unusually combines the patience, and conservatism of Saturn and the innovative spirit, futurism and adventurism of Uranus. Uranus is a planet of space, science, aviation and technical revolutions. So Aquarius often work in these fields. Aquarius is good at shocking people and making experiments. They run far ahead of their time shocking others. Aquarius is heralds of the future.They are amazing forecasters and always generate new ideas.

It is important for Aquarius to have a company of like-minded people and friends. They are able to unite others to transfer their ideas into reality. You should never push Aquarius telling them: “you must”. If you say this to Aquarius, they are most likely run away from you. The word “must” causes hysteria for Aquarius. The principle of voluntariness is very important for them. Aquarius can seem crazy, unpredictable, incomprehensible to many people, but you definitely won't be bored with them.
