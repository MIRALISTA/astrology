✡ Taurus ✡

Taurus is the second of the 12 signs of the zodiac. Element: Earth. That is the flesh of our whole world. It is the material basis which organizes the activities of the other three elements. Ruling Planet: Venus. Taureans always look representative and are usually taller than others. People of this sign seem to be restrained, calm and slow like they live in their own world and the rest of world is not interesting for them. They are great lovers of comfort and expensive things.

They have really excellent qualities: a sense of taste, love of beauty and aesthetics. Taurus and money are always nearby. They know how to make money, and money likes Taurus too.Taureans’ house is their fortress. Taureans equip their home with great love. And the more money they have, the more luxurious their home will be. Taurus is also a good family man, loyal and devoted to his family. People of this sign are very hospitable, like to treat guests with expensive wines.

But there are two Taureans’ extremes. One of them is creative, divine bull that creates gold around. And the second is the Minotaur (the Minotaur is a mythical scary creature with a human body and a bull's head). Taurus can be very patient for a long time while being in crisis situations or in the situation when he doesn't like something. But if you bring the Taureans of their wrath, they can be so powerful that it is better not to see it! They become furious and instead of an intelligent, respectable person the Minotaur can appear which easily will swallow you. If Taureans get sick, the best way to recover is to go to nature. Nature and fresh air heals them magically.

This is one of the most hardy and stubborn of the signs. The more they suffer and fate, the stronger physically and mentally they become. Career is moving slowly, but steadily, not seeking for easy ways. And eventually they become very successful.
