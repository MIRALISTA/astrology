✡ Astrology ✡

From a mathematical point of view, astrology is the exact science which means that the actual success of reading a horoscope card completely depends on the correct calculation. For more accurate horoscope you need to know your place, exact date and time of birth, preferably the exact minute (that can be quite difficult sometimes for any reasons). Palmistry in this regard, has a number of advantages. A palmist doesn`t need to know the exact time of birth, because a palm built by nature through the lines and signs on the hand in this case is like a Natal Chart for astrology . The condition of hills, lines on your hands shows the character, opportunities and events in a person's life. For more reliable forecast we combine palmistry and astrology, which gives us the opportunity to consider in more detail of the fate of a man.
