✡ Cancer child ✡

All children without exception need attention, care and love. But in particular, Child Cancer needs this. children are extremely emotional. They are the most sensitive, the most tender, and the most unprotected children of all the signs of the zodiac. They can look at this world with great fear and horror.

Advice to parents: Under no circumstances should the child be frightened. He/she is already terrified of this life. Such children often have a sad, mysterious look. Girls cry a lot. Therefore, only the words of approval, support and love should be heard by such a child. All of your words are deposited in his soul and create his own image about the things that you told them. Otherwise, you will break such a child, his psyche will be broken for life.

You do not need to scream, scold such a child, because if you frown, it's enough that your child understands that he/she did something wrong. In order for the cancer to be more independent, give them a task that they must perform themselves (collecting pyramid from cubes, collecting something from the design, cleaning the toys, etc.) and then, after that, praise them.

Cancer’s is a fortress for such children, so parents must create this protection. Cancers should know that next to them there are their friends, loving parents who will always protect. If a does not want to communicate with a person, never force to do it. Communication with unloved people has a bad effect on his emotional state, such a child can even get sick physically. Such a child came into this life with peace, in order to receive warmth and kindness from you. You must become a friend for your small cancer and should be very supportive parents. As a result, your child will have a very good and happy future.
