✡ Capricorn child ✡

Infants of Capricorn sign do not give parents unnecessary worries. They can lie quietly for hours, even if the diapers are soaked. Capricorn does not require much attention. If they are feed with something tasty, it is nice, if they are given with ordinary food, it is also not bad for them. They are happy with those toys that were bought a few months ago and don't need something new every day.

️Little Capricorns being the children of Saturn can look at the world rather pessimistically. They have a suffering, dreamy, mystical soul. ️Advice to parents - talk more with your little Capricorn, ask about their life, what is interesting for them, what causes emotional discomfort for them. A Capricorn child should know that they will always be listened to, understood and helped. Otherwise, if there was no good relationship in childhood, such a child in adolescence may become very closed. If Capricorn decided to share with you some information, never turn this information against them, even if the information shocked you. Make a dialogue with Capricorn - they need it more than anyone else. Particular attention should be paid to youthful years. The fact that parents did not notice psychological problems can lead to tragedies. As Capricorn child has the quite pessimistic nature it lead that there are a lot of children’s suicides among Capricorn. Depression is perhaps one of the main and leading features of Capricorn. Emotionally, children are not stable. Therefore, you need to pay attention to their psychological background carefully. Try to become a friend for your child, look for an approach to them. Considering the fact that such a child a pessimist is from their birth, the atmosphere in the house should be smiling, joyful and harmonious. It will gives strength and optimism to such a child. ️It is no needed to drive Capricorn child guests, force them to read poetry in public, to play group games, if they don’t want to do this - this is not the natural environment of the little

The early years are often difficult and full of struggle for them. Capricorn adults are very serious, practical, agile and stubborn in achieving their goal. They are very disciplined and require the same from others. Capricorn are professionals in what they do, but they can be terribly meticulous and boring. They can bring freedom-loved and informal Aquarius or Sagittarius to a nervous tic, while not paying attention to their feelings, because the rules exist to follow them!

Capricorns are prudent, economical (these qualities even can turn into greed). Luxurious life is not for Capricorn. They love such a lifestyle in order to have a stable, may be small, but guaranteed income. They will not take a risk, because it can undermine their material well-being. Therefore, they can refuse from good business offers and misses their chance, because they are afraid of losing what they have. They achieve their goals working hard. They set tasks for themselves that can only be solved independently by themselves. The more complicated the goal, the more determined and active they are in order to reach it.

All mental emotions and impulses of Capricornare under strict control. They often have the lack of sensuality and tenderness. Capricorn has all these qualities, but they don't demonstrate them for public - they have such a nature. They are often shy and sensitive people inside who can suddenly fall into melancholy, succumb to despair, depression. And they compensate for the lack of sensuality with loyalty and reliability.

Capricorn can be quite slow, but you do not need to make them hurry. Capricorn child is smart, talented with a good memory. Capricorn child like to read, but they are not capable of quick, decisive action. Therefore, they just need to help open up. And do not worry that child is slow. They are much faster than other signs can reach the goal. There are signs that do everything quickly, but superficially and frivolously. And Capricorn may do not so quickly, but confidently and responsibly.

If Capricorn child for some reason decided that they are not good in a subject at school, it would be difficult to change their mind. You need to be patient and try to tactfully explain them that everything is possible, they just need to spend more time for studying. The main thing is not to laugh at your child if they don't understand something. You need become a friend and helper for them.

Another feature of Capricorn is “small adults”. When you communicate with a small person, talk to them as an equal and remember that little Capricorn is an adult inside. And due to that fact, they like to communicate with adults. Capricorn would rather sit on the bench with their grandparents, rather than run with the children on the playground.

️You should pay attention to the health of the little Capricorn. Any illness in childhood, starting from infancy and up to 14-15 years old, can become a serious chronic problem. Capricorn is not very stable for all sorts of illness up to 15 years old. Teach your child to do any sports. This will help strengthen their immunity. Show your love, care, attention, patience and you will only be proud of your child in adulthood.
