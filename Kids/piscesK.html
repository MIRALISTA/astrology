✡ Pisces child ✡

The angel-looking baby born under this sign is not really an angel: despite the charming face, they can achieve their goals not by crying like a Aries child, or stubbornness like a Taurus, but with a smile and cute tricks.️ Having barely learned to walk, Pisces begins to live in a magical world of fantasy. If a mother needs to do laundry or cleaning, it is best for her to inflate several balloons, surround the baby with their favorite soft toys, play music and tell the child that they are in a circus. Then the child will not interfere with it.

The most difficult thing in raising Pisces is to teach them to control a certain daily routine, as they tend to eat and sleep only when they like it. The child can have problems with timing. So, little Pisces are often late. Therefore, the child must be organized from early childhood. But don't make your voice higher in order to increase the level of discipline, because then the Pisces child feels danger from you and can become very closed to themselves. Another feature of a small Pisces is that they tend to break away from reality and exist in a world of fictional illusions. From childhood, Pisces must be helped in the selection of clear moral positions: “what is good and what is bad” should be explained by parents clearly. Then in adolescence the Pisces will have less chance getting into bad company.

These children are often accused of lying. But Pisces do not lie - they just have such a rich imagination that they sometimes confuse fiction with reality. These children think their own way. Therefore, instead of punishing them, it is better to direct their imaginations in a creative direction - let them compose poems, draw or play roles in drama group plays. Try to protect their talents and surround them with your love for as long as possible. Try to provide positive and friendly atmosphere in your home for your Pisces.Teach them to be stable and brave.

Sometimes Pisces child tend to feel unreasonably upset. They are very vulnerable and can cry very often. Raising a child, be softer and more tolerant. Always remember how sensitive they are. Teach your baby to use innate intuition - it will help them in their whole life. Explain that this is a priceless gift that can be well-being developed with skillful handling.

Pisces children need your permanent attention and support. Try to do something with your baby and let it become a tradition for you. Surround the child with warmth and love constantly and be tolerant to their sensitivity. All your good feelings will return to you in abundance. Create appropriate conditions for development of your child and your Pisces will have opportunities to become as talented as Elizabeth Taylor, Frideric Chopin, S. Mikhoels, Rudolf Nureyev, George Washington, John Steinbeck, Handel, Victor Hugo, Enrico Caruso, Michelangelo Buonarotti, Albert Einstein, N. Rimsky-Korsakov, Auguste Renoir.
